Experience of London
Liveability refers to the overall experience and quality of life in a city. There are many positive and negative dimensions that influence liveability. This project explores a selection of invisible liveability patterns related to working and playing in London.

Four facets that uniquely influence the liveability of London are investigated. Two relate to the working experience - London’s gender pay gap and the temporal rhythms of the tube network. The second two facets relate to the leisure experience - London’s extraordinary social events and the importance of pub culture.
